2. How Doorstep Service Works

Step 1: Book Appointment

Contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or website form. Provide device details and preferred time slot.

Step 2: Confirmation

We confirm your appointment with technician details, estimated arrival time, and preliminary quote.

Step 3: Technician Arrival

Certified technician arrives at your location with tools and quality parts within agreed time window.

Step 4: Device Assessment

Quick diagnostic check and final price confirmation before starting any work.

Step 5: On-Site Repair

Complete repair at your location while you watch. Most repairs take 30-90 minutes.

Step 6: Testing & Handover

Thorough testing of all functions, payment, warranty certificate, and device handover.
